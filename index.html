A Little About Me

Hello! I'm Shreya

I started tutoring 6 years ago, at the age of 14. What started out as helping my classmates with math, chemistry and physics soon turned into my lifelong passion project.

I am currently a student at Purdue University, studying Computer Science and Mathematics. I truly believe that every learner is special, and have made it my mission to help every learner that comes my way see how wonderful they are.

Mathematics

From Algebra to Calculus, I have impacted over 100 students by uniquely combining theoretical concepts with hands-on activities and visualization techniques. I have tutored students ranging from 6 to 18 years old in topics like algebra, geometry, calculus and SAT test prep.
